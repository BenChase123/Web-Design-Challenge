Latitude Vs X Variable

The purpose of this webpage is to analyze weather changes as you move away from the equator towards the north or south pole. To gather all our weather data spread across the world, we utilized OpenWeather API calls to 1000 cities with totally random locations.

After collecting all the cities and the weather data, we used matplotlib to show scatter plots of latitude vs temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and windiness. We analyzed any discernable correlations and came up with possible explanations for given weather phenomena relating to temperature, humidity, windiness, or cloudiness.
